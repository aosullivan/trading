## Trust Boundaries

- The design must remain traceable to the transcript principles and the Phase 3 diagnostics, or it becomes an ungrounded optimization exercise.
- Candidate ranking is a decision input for Phase 5; if it is vague or hand-wavy, the implementation phase will sprawl.
- The new sleeve model must preserve meaningful exposure on strong trends; designs that quietly recreate tiny risk-budget sizing fail the phase objective.
